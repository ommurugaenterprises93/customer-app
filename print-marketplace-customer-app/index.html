Cancellation & Refund Policy

Cancellation Policy

We believe in giving our customers flexibility. Our cancellation rules are automatically enforced in our production pipeline:

  • Eligible for Cancel: Customers can cancel orders directly from their tracking dashboard at any time during the Placed, Payment Confirmed, or Vendor Accepted phases. Cancellation instantly triggers a full refund.
  • Ineligible for Cancel: Once the print vendor marks the order as Printing, the physical print process has begun. The cancel button will automatically lock, and order cancellation is no longer possible.

Refund & Product Issues Policy

Since prints are custom manufactured to specifications, refunds are only issued if the delivered product does not match your upload or specifications.

  • Refund requests can only be initiated on orders that have reached the Delivered or Collected status.
  • To request a refund, go to My Orders, locate the order, and click the Report Wrong Product button.
  • You must fill out the form specifying the exact issue and upload a photograph demonstrating the defect/error.
  • Once submitted, the order shifts to a Refund Requested status for vendor administration review.
